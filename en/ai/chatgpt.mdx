Vendaze is available as a connector in ChatGPT. You can connect your Vendaze account directly from the ChatGPT interface, no technical setup required.

## How to connect

1. Open [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) and start a new conversation
2. Click the **Connect apps** or **+** button in the chat input area
3. Search for **Vendaze** and click **Connect**
4. You will be redirected to Vendaze to authorize access to your workspace
5. Select the workspace you want to connect and confirm the requested scopes
6. Done. ChatGPT now has access to your Vendaze account.

## What you can do

With Vendaze connected, you can talk to ChatGPT in natural language:

**Queries:**

- "What are my open deals this month and what is their total value?"
- "Show me the contacts added in the last 7 days."
- "Which deals haven't been updated in more than 2 weeks?"
- "List all overdue tasks assigned to me."

**Actions:**

- "Create a contact named Maria Souza at Empresa ABC with email maria@abc.com."
- "Add a note to the 'Annual License Renewal' deal saying the proposal was sent today."
- "Create a task to follow up on the proposal next Monday."
- "Log a meeting activity on the Acme Corp deal with note 'Presentation completed, awaiting internal approval'."

## Managing the connection

To disconnect or reconnect your Vendaze account, click the connector icon in ChatGPT and manage the Vendaze connection from there.

<Note>
  ChatGPT accesses only the workspace you authorized during the connection flow. To switch
  workspaces, disconnect and reconnect selecting the desired workspace.
</Note>
