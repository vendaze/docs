Vendaze is listed as an integration in Claude. You can connect your Vendaze account directly from the Claude apps panel, no technical setup required.

## How to connect

1. Open [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and go to **Settings > Integrations** (or the apps panel in Claude Desktop)
2. Search for **Vendaze** and click **Connect**
3. You will be redirected to Vendaze to authorize access to your workspace
4. Select the workspace you want to connect and confirm the requested scopes
5. Done. Claude now has access to your Vendaze account.

## What you can do

With Vendaze connected, you can talk to Claude in natural language:

**Queries:**

- "What are the 10 highest-value deals in the Commercial pipeline?"
- "Show me the contacts at Acme Corp with all their information."
- "Which tasks are open with an overdue date?"
- "List the activities on the Acme Corp deal from the last two weeks."

**Actions:**

- "Create a contact named John Smith, at company XYZ, with email john@xyz.com."
- "Move the deal 'Quarterly Contract' to the 'Proposal Sent' stage."
- "Add a note on contact Ana Costa saying she returns on the 15th."
- "Create a task to call the customer tomorrow at 2pm, linked to the Quarterly Contract deal."

## Managing the connection

To disconnect or reconnect your Vendaze account, go to **Settings > Integrations** in Claude and manage the Vendaze connection from there.

<Note>
  Claude accesses only the workspace you authorized during the connection flow. To switch
  workspaces, disconnect and reconnect selecting the desired workspace.
</Note>
