Vendaze is available as an integration in Manus. Once connected, your Manus agent can read and update your CRM data as part of automated tasks and multi-step workflows.

## How to connect

1. Open [manus.im](https://manus.im) and go to the **Integrations** panel
2. Search for **Vendaze** and click **Connect**
3. You will be redirected to Vendaze to authorize access to your workspace
4. Select the workspace you want to connect and confirm the requested scopes
5. Done. Manus now has access to your Vendaze account.

## What your agent can do

With Vendaze connected, your Manus agent can act on your CRM as part of complex tasks:

**Examples:**

- "Every morning, summarize the deals updated yesterday and send me a message."
- "When I paste a LinkedIn profile, create a contact in Vendaze with the data and create a task to reach out in 2 days."
- "Research company Acme Corp online and fill in the missing fields in our Vendaze record for them."
- "Look at all open deals with no activity in the last 10 days and draft a follow-up email for each one."
- "Create contacts in bulk from the data in this spreadsheet."

## Managing the connection

To disconnect or reconnect your Vendaze account, go to the **Integrations** panel in Manus and manage the Vendaze connection from there.

<Note>
  Manus accesses only the workspace you authorized during the connection flow. To switch workspaces,
  disconnect and reconnect selecting the desired workspace.
</Note>
