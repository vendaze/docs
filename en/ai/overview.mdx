Vendaze integrates natively with AI assistants and automation platforms. Connect your account once and let your tools interact with your CRM directly, with no code required.

## AI assistants

The Vendaze MCP (Model Context Protocol) server is listed as an official integration in three platforms. Connect your account and start asking questions and performing actions in your CRM through natural conversation.

Once connected, you can talk to your assistant naturally:

- "What are my open deals this month and what is their total value?"
- "Create a contact named John Smith at company XYZ with email john@xyz.com."
- "Which tasks are overdue?"
- "Move the Quarterly Contract deal to the Proposal Sent stage."

For the full list of available actions, see the [MCP Server reference](/en/ai/mcp).
