Vendaze has an official app on Zapier. Any Vendaze customer can connect their account directly from the Zapier platform, no developer credentials required.

## How to connect

1. Go to [zapier.com/apps/vendaze/integrations](https://zapier.com/apps/vendaze/integrations)
2. Click **Connect Vendaze**
3. You will be redirected to Vendaze to authorize access to your workspace
4. Select the workspace you want to connect and confirm the requested scopes
5. Done. Your Vendaze account is connected and ready to use in Zaps.

## Configure a Zap

After connecting, choose the trigger and action:

**Available triggers** (what starts the Zap):

- New contact created
- Contact updated
- New company created
- New deal created
- Deal updated
- New task created

**Available actions** (what the Zap does):

- Create contact
- Update contact
- Create company
- Create deal
- Update deal
- Create task
- Log activity

## Example automations

**Form to CRM:**
When someone fills out a Typeform form, automatically create a contact in Vendaze with the submitted data.

**CRM to spreadsheet:**
When a deal moves to the "Won" stage, add a row in Google Sheets with the deal data.

**Email to task:**
When an email tagged with a specific label arrives in Gmail, create a follow-up task in Vendaze.

## Notes

- Zapier checks for new events every 5 to 15 minutes, depending on your Zapier plan
- Workspace custom fields appear as optional fields in creation and update actions
- To reconnect or manage permissions, go to **My Apps** in Zapier and find the Vendaze connection
