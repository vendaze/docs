## Where to start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register your app">
    Get your `client_id` and `client_secret` in minutes. No approval required, no waiting.

    [Register your app](/en/guides/register-app)

  </Step>
  <Step title="Implement authentication">
    Add the OAuth 2.1 flow with PKCE to authorize Vendaze users in your product.

    [Authentication guide](/en/guides/authentication)

  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your first request">
    Use your `access_token` to call `GET /v1/people` and retrieve workspace contacts.

    [First request](/en/guides/first-request)

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Base URL

```
https://api.vendaze.com
```

All endpoints are versioned under `/v1`.
